About SERA Bangladesh

Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Dhaka, SERA Bangladesh is a leading organization for sustainable development, empowering a diverse clientele—government entities, non-profits, private businesses, and individuals—through its multifaceted consulting services. Our unwavering commitment extends across the economic and social landscape, fostering collaboration for positive change.

We distinguish ourselves through our cutting-edge approach, leveraging the transformative power of the Internet of Things (IoT) and traceability solutions to drive efficiency and transparency. Our comprehensive suite of services encompasses project management across various sectors, digital transformation consulting, and custom software development. Furthermore, SERA Bangladesh offers expertise in sustainability consulting, strategic planning, and food security. We support non-profits with capacity building and program design, and bridge the gap to funding. Notably, we assist with organic certification, agricultural research, and market entry for both domestic and foreign businesses. SERA Bangladesh further fuels progress through educational programs on critical sustainability topics.
